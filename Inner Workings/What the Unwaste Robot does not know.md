# What the Unwaste Robot does not know

Some values are inherently limited by what the installation measures.

Unwaste Robot does not know:

* energy usage during STOPPED periods,
* how devices would have behaved without control (counterfactual),
* unmetered production or consumption hidden by incomplete metering,
* user intent (for example, why a device ran at a given time).

These limits do not mean results are wrong, but they explain why:

* some values are estimated,
* residuals may appear,
* and some results depend on installation completeness.
